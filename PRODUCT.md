# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Aspiring illustrators, fashion-design students, and serious hobbyists — in India and the West — who want to draw garments and figures on paper. They arrive curious and convert when they see (a) clear skill, (b) that it's teachable in steps, and (c) a track that matches their level. Primary fear to defuse: "I can't draw well enough to start."

## Product Purpose

A marketing landing page for Poojitha / Orpu Art House — a working fashion illustrator who teaches graphite + colored-pencil illustration. Phase 1 goal: convert visitors to Instagram DM leads ("CLASSES"). Every section earns trust through the artwork itself. Phase 2 (later): same site becomes a paid online class platform with gated video lessons, region-based pricing, and topic picker.

## Brand Personality

Editorial. Gallery-calm. Quietly authoritative.

Voice: warm, encouraging, confident — never salesy. The artwork speaks loudest; copy supports it. Feels like a studio visit, not a sales funnel.

## Anti-references

- Generic online-course platforms (Skillshare, Udemy aesthetic) — bright, badge-heavy, transactional
- SaaS-cream / warm-neutral AI defaults — the linen-parchment-wheat palette with DM Sans and Cormorant is already the AI tell; texture must feel intentional, not scaffolded
- Gradient text, glassmorphism, numbered eyebrows on every section
- Anything that competes visually with the artwork — the art is the only loud element

## Design Principles

1. **Art-first.** Every layout decision defers to the artwork. White space is not emptiness — it's the frame.
2. **Show, don't pitch.** Trust is built by the work, not the copy. Process footage and before→after beats any testimonial.
3. **Calm authority.** No urgency mechanics, no countdown timers. The tone is a working illustrator inviting you in.
4. **Phase-2 ready without Phase-2 clutter.** Offer and pricing sections exist as clearly-marked placeholders — not hidden, not half-built, not awkward.
5. **One action.** Every CTA funnels to the same Instagram DM. No decision fatigue.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum (body text ≥ 4.5:1, large text ≥ 3:1)
- All autoplay video: muted, no sound, reduced-motion respected
- Artwork images: descriptive alt text for screen readers
- International audience (India + West): no currency or region assumptions in v1 UI
